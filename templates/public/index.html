GopherCon 2017 will be in Denver, Colorado again in 2017. The conference starts on July 13th, with an optional workshop day on the 12th. Our final day the 15th is the "Community Day".

The Largest Go Conference in the World

GopherCon is the original Go conference.

Hosted each summer in Denver, Colorado, GopherCon started in 2014, with an amazing first year attendance of over 700 people. Since then, we've doubled in attendance. We expect to sell out early this year, with a maximum capacity of 1500 people. Don't wait for the last minute to buy your tickets. We'd hate for you to be sad.

Speakers

GopherCon is proud of our tradition of bringing exciting topics from a diverse range of speakers to the big stage. Each year, we strive to spotlight new and interesting topics from a wide variety of speakers to create a program that is rich, interesting, and diverse. If you have a story to tell, a lesson to teach, or an exciting technique to share, we welcome your proposals. Accepted speakers will be notified in February.

About GopherCon

GopherCon, It is the largest event in the world dedicated solely to the Go programming language. It's attended by the best and the brightest of the Go team and community. Join us as 1,500 gophers converge on Denver, Colorado for 3 full days of talks, events, & hacking.
